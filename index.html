Hi today we will be discussing about the followings, Latte, Macchiato and Expresso.

Latte

A latte is a popular coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a small amount of milk foam. The ratio of milk to espresso is high, giving it a creamy texture and mild coffee flavor. Lattes are often enjoyed with flavorings like vanilla, caramel, or hazelnut, and can be served hot or iced.

Macchiato

Macchiato is an Italian coffee drink that means "stained" or "spotted" in Italian. It's typically made with a small amount of espresso and a small amount of milk, giving it a stronger coffee flavor compared to drinks with more milk, like lattes or cappuccinos.

Expresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans at high pressure. It forms the base for many popular coffee drinks and is known for its rich, intense flavor and creamy consistency.
